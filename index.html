NovaQuest Discord Rules

These rules are designed to keep our Discord community safe, fair, and enjoyable for everyone. All members are expected to follow these rules, and staff will enforce them consistently. Ignorance of the rules will not be accepted as an excuse.

  • Be respectful to all members, staff, and moderators.
  • No harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discriminatory language.
  • No spamming, excessive caps, or flooding channels.
  • No advertising or promoting other servers, services, or websites.
  • Follow staff instructions at all times. Ignoring staff can result in strikes or removal.
  • No sharing of personal information of yourself or others (doxxing).
  • No threats, harassment, or encouragement of illegal activity.
  • No posting of malicious links, viruses, or files.
  • No impersonating staff or other members in a way that causes confusion or harm.
  • No NSFW content in channels not marked for NSFW.
  • No offensive images, text, or media in any public channel.
  • Do not share pirated or illegal content.
  • Respect channel topics. Keep conversations relevant.
  • No harassment, shouting, or trolling other users in voice channels.
  • No playing loud music or sound effects that disrupt conversation without permission.
  • Follow staff directions regarding voice channel usage.
  • No spamming bot commands or abusing bot systems.
  • No using self-bots or automation to gain unfair advantages.
  • Follow instructions for bot usage and channel-specific bot commands.
  • Breaking any rule may result in a warning, mute, kick, temporary ban, or permanent ban depending on severity.
  • Repeated offenses will result in longer punishments.
  • Serious security violations (doxxing, threats, malicious links) will result in immediate ban and report to Discord Trust & Safety.
  • All punishments are logged and may be reviewed by the management team for appeals or consistency.
