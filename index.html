As the founder of NovaQuest, I am committed to making all relevant information public to the community. This decision comes from my experience as staff on multiple servers, where founders were not transparent and rules were created on the fly. NovaQuest will be different.

By sharing information openly, both staff and players can build trust and work together effectively. All decisions regarding the community—including in-game rules, staff expectations, punishments, and appeals—will be public. This ensures:

  • Players know exactly what to expect from staff
  • Punishments are clear and consistent
  • Appeals are visible, showing the second chances we strive to provide

Our goal is to make rules and policies as clear as possible, with no loopholes, while maintaining fairness. Everyone makes mistakes, and this approach allows the community to see progress, accountability, and the efforts we make to keep NovaQuest safe, fair, and enjoyable for all.

NovaQuest Staff Rules & Expectations

This staff guide is publicly available to ensure transparency, clearly outlining staff expectations and allowing players to submit feedback to management if a staff member is believed to be in violation of these guidelines.

  • Prioritize staff responsibilities over gameplay.
  • Maintain professionalism in all interactions.
  • Follow hierarchy rules.
  • Do not hint at promotions.
  • Report bugs within 24 hours; exploiting unreported bugs is a strikeable offense.
  • Do not leak confidential information (staff tickets, staff Discord, internal discussions).
  • Helpers: Warn, Kick, Mute
  • JrMods: Warn, Kick, Mute, Ban, System Access
  • Mods: Guide Helpers & JrMods, reverse punishments if necessary
  • SrMods: Mentor lower staff, senior moderation, advanced permissions
  • Admins+: Full permissions, confidential commands, leadership oversight

NovaQuest In-Game Rules

General Policy

  • Ignorance of rules is not an excuse.
  • Loopholes cannot be exploited.
  • Staff may punish harmful behavior not explicitly listed.
  • Punishments escalate based on offense history and severity.

Unfair Advantage / Competitive Violations

  • Use of hacked clients or macros is prohibited.
  • AFK automation, boosting, X-Ray, Fly, KillAura, etc. are banned.
  • Regular players: progressive bans (first offense → 3d, second → 7d, third → 14d).
  • Competitive: 3d ban + stat wipe + leaderboard removal + season ban.

IRL Trading & Gambling

  • Trading or selling anything outside NovaQuest is prohibited.
  • Player-run casinos or gambling are not allowed.
  • NovaQuest-run events with EULA compliance are allowed.

NovaQuest Discord Rules

  • Be respectful; no harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discrimination.
  • No spamming, excessive caps, or flooding channels.
  • No advertising other servers.
  • Follow staff instructions at all times.
  • No harassment, bullying, or discrimination in voice channels.
  • Keep conversations respectful and appropriate for all audiences.
